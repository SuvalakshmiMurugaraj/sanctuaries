Wildlife Sanctuaries In Andhra Pradesh

Sanctuaries

1.Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary

Location: Coringa, East Godavari District, Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh

Situated approximately 18 kilometers from the coastal city of Kakinada, the Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary in Andhra Pradesh stands as one of the prominent wildlife sanctuaries in the region. Renowned for its extensive mangrove ecosystems and dry deciduous forests, this sanctuary is a haven for diverse avian species, as well as small fishes and shrimps. It boasts a recorded count of over 120 bird species, along with a varied wildlife population that includes the golden jackal, fishing cat, and sea turtle, contributing to its ecological significance.

2.Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary

Location: Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

For those in the vicinity of Visakhapatnam seeking a wildlife sanctuary experience, the Kambala Konda Wildlife Sanctuary presents an excellent choice. Encompassing an expansive area of 70.7 square kilometers, this sanctuary is characterized by dry evergreen forests, interspersed with diverse scrubs and meadows, rendering it one of Andhra Pradesh's most renowned wildlife sanctuaries. Visitors have the opportunity to encounter various animals, including the Indian muntjac, leopard, boar, jungle cat, sambar deer, spotted deer, and numerous others. Additionally, the sanctuary boasts a rich array of reptiles and bird species, enhancing its appeal to nature enthusiasts.

3.Rollapadu Wildlife Sanctuary

Location: Rollapadu, Andhra Pradesh

Situated approximately 40 kilometers from Kurnool, the Rollapadu Wildlife Sanctuary of Andhra Pradesh stands out as one of the most renowned wildlife sanctuaries in Andhra Pradesh. It is particularly famous for being the habitat of the Great Indian Bustard, an exceptionally rare bird species. Visitors are drawn not only to the diverse animal life but also to the breathtaking spectacle of migratory birds that converge here from various directions. Spanning an area of 6.14 square kilometers, this sanctuary was established in 1988 with the primary aim of conserving the dwindling population of the Indian Bustard. The landscape is dominated by dry grasslands, characterized by unique vegetation distinct from other options. Rollapadu also serves as a crucial base site for numerous migratory bird species. Besides the avian inhabitants, the sanctuary is home to various animals, including deer, blackbucks, foxes, bonnet macaques, jackals, sloth bears, and even jungle cats, enriching the overall wildlife experience.

4.Sri Venkateswara Wildlife Sanctuary

Location: KT Road, near Nandi Statue Circle, Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh

When visiting Tirumala and seeking exploration beyond, the Sri Venkateswara Wildlife Sanctuary in Andhra Pradesh, a mere 15 kilometers from the city, beckons as a must-visit destination. As one of Andhra Pradesh's largest wildlife sanctuaries spanning 353 square kilometers, it offers a verdant haven. Established in 1989, the sanctuary boasts dense and lush greenery, creating an enchanting setting. Its rich biodiversity includes tiger, blackbuck, panther, spotted deer, bonnet monkey, bison, fox, jackal, and an array of migratory birds. Additionally, the sanctuary provides numerous trekking trails with two distinct entry points, inviting adventurers to explore its natural wonders.

5.Nagarjuna Sagar Srisailam Sanctuary

Location: Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh

If you're looking to explore a remarkable wildlife sanctuary straddling the border of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, the Nagarjuna Sagar Srisailam Sanctuary emerges as a top choice. Located approximately 60 kilometers from Nagarjuna Sagar, it offers an exceptional wildlife experience. As the largest tiger reserve in the country, it spans 3,568 square kilometers and is situated between the Nagarjunasagar and Srisailam reservoirs. Beyond its majestic tiger population, the sanctuary is home to panthers, spotted deer, sambars, nilgai, wolves, and a variety of other wildlife species. Embarking on a 1.5-hour jeep safari, priced at around INR 800 per person, promises an unforgettable adventure well worth the investment.
